Essay Contest

This is an opportunity to put your writing skills to the test and stand a chance to win a sum of K200. The contest is for senior secondary students who want to improve there writing skills, creativity and research skills. Geocademy believes in the ability of every learner to be a great future leader and this competition is one way of sharing our values with the nation.



Reward

The winner of this competition will be awarded a sum of K200 and will be put on our website and social media platforms

Rules & Regulations

  1. The judges decision on who wins is final.
  2. Your essay must have a title which is underlined or in block letters.
  3. Incorrect spellings attracts loss o marks.
  4. Your essay must not have words more than 300.
  5. All essays must be submitted in the time given.
  6. If your essay is typed make sure your title is undelined
